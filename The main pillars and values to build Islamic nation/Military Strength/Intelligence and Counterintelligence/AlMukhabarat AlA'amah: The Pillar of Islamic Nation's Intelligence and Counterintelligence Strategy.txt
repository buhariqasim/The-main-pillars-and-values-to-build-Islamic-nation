# Al-Mukhabarat Al-A'amah: The Pillar of Islamic Nation's Intelligence and Counterintelligence Strategy

In essence, Al-Mukhabarat Al-A'amah works tirelessly to protect the Islamic Nation and its citizens from international threats.

In these endeavors, Al-Mukhabarat Al-A'amah often implements advanced technology, maintains empowered manpower, and makes use of its extraordinary capacity for unconventional warfare.
